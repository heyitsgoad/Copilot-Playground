# Positivity Prompts

Try these different positivity prompts to help get a little boost in your day and also help you realize your impact on things you are working on and coworkers you are supporting!

**Important:** You’ll need a Microsoft Copilot Premium license for this, and you’ll need to do it on the work side of Copilot.

---

## Prompt 1

After everything we have discussed together and all that you know about me, what's one thing about me that I may not know or realize

---

## Prompt 2

Act like a close friend, someone who's been in my corner for a long time— who's seen my wins, my struggles, and my patterns, I ask for honesty, not comfort. Based on everything you know from our past interactions, including my mail and team chats, tell me 10 things I need to hear right now that will keep me motivated. For each one, explain why you're saying it, link it directly to a pattern, habit, strength you've seen in me before. Be honest. Be direct, Be specific

---

## Prompt 3

Looking back at this week: What did I accomplish that made a difference? What moments brought me energy or pride? What did I learn or improve? Who did I support or connect with meaningfully? Looking ahead: What am I excited to explore or build next week? What one intention will guide me into Monday with clarity and purpose?
